*January 7, 2026 – Las Vegas, NV*

Today at CES, the global event dedicated to technology breakthroughs and innovation, Adapt announced the launch of its new Creator Portal as part of a series of strategic investments designed to expand localization services for the creator and digital economy, in addition to serving legacy media companies.

The Creator Portal (adaptglobal.io/creators) provides creators with access to high-quality, fast localization at an affordable price. By combining the latest AI technology with Adapt's network of linguistically trained Cultural Ambassadors, the platform ensures translated dubs resonate authentically with global audiences.

> "Traditional media and the creator economy are on a collision course," said Adapt CEO Justin Beaudin. "We aim to serve both markets, and we're excited to see creators increasingly value high-quality translations and dubs so viewers around the world can enjoy their content in the same way they do content from major studios."

Creators who want to reach new audiences in their local markets can apply to the Global Launch Program. If accepted, Adapt covers all localization costs in exchange for a revenue-sharing arrangement on global earnings.

The Creator Portal aligns with Adapt's broader mission to integrate human expertise with advanced AI technology to elevate media services and make high-quality global content accessible to audiences everywhere. Creators who join the portal become part of a growing community helping shape the future of international content experiences.

## About Adapt

Founded in 2024, Adapt is a next-generation localization service provider that merges AI-driven transcription, translation, and voice generation with regional expertise to deliver high-quality dubs and subtitles for premium content — at half the cost and twice the speed of traditional providers.

Adapt's mission is to integrate AI with art and humans with technology, making content accessible to global audiences everywhere.
