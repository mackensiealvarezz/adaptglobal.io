*July 9, 2025 – Denver, CO*

Adapt Global Studios is honored to be included in the 2025 Slator Language AI "50 Under 50", a list that recognizes the "most exciting and active companies deploying language AI." Adapt is featured among the 50 firms that are "shaping the future of multilingual communication" across the world.

The list, curated by Slator — a leading source of news and analysis for the global language industry — recognizes that language AI startups are "showing increasing differentiation" and are "emerging as vertical specialists, moving beyond core language functionalities to deliver 'whole product' experiences that solve problems for buyers."

Adapt stood out for its Nuance platform, a proprietary cloud-native ecosystem that integrates nearly two dozen AI engines for transcription, translation, voice generation, and lip-sync dubbing — all guided by a trained global workforce of Cultural Ambassadors.

> "We're proud to be included in this year's Language AI 50," said Justin Beaudin, CEO of Adapt. "It's our mission to combine human expertise with the latest AI tools to make content more accessible worldwide."

Read the full list at [slator.com](https://slator.com/2025-slator-language-ai-50-under-50).

## About Adapt

Founded in 2024, Adapt is a next-generation localization service provider that merges AI-driven transcription, translation, and voice generation with regional experts to deliver high-quality dubs and subtitles for premium content — at half the cost and twice the speed of traditional providers.
