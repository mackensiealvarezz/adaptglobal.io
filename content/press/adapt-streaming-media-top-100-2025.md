*December 15, 2025 – Denver, CO*

Adapt Global Studios has been recognized in Streaming Media's 2025 Top 100, a list highlighting the most innovative and influential companies shaping the streaming media industry. Adapt is featured among the 100 firms "driving the evolution of content delivery and streaming technology," alongside leading global companies including The Walt Disney Company and Apple.

The Top 100 list, curated by Streaming Media Magazine, celebrates companies that are "pushing the boundaries of streaming innovation, accessibility, and audience engagement." Adapt stood out for its Nuance platform, a cloud-native ecosystem that integrates transcription, translation, voice generation, and lip-sync dubbing — powered by a trained global workforce of Cultural Ambassadors.

> "We are honored to be included in Streaming Media's Top 100," said Justin Beaudin, Founder and CEO of Adapt. "It's a recognition of the great work both our technology team and our global linguistics team are doing to push the boundaries of what's possible in media localization."

## About Adapt

Adapt is a next-generation localization service provider that merges AI-driven transcription, translation, and voice generation with regional expertise to deliver high-quality dubs and subtitles for premium content — at half the cost and twice the speed of traditional providers.

Adapt's mission is to integrate AI with art and humans with technology, making content accessible to global audiences at the quality consumers demand and the efficiency creators need. We turn the acceptable into the exceptional.
