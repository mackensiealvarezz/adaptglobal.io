*February 13, 2026 – Denver, CO*

Adapt, a next generation localization company, has engaged human linguists to deliver high quality AI-enabled multilingual dubs for Flashback Pictures' vertical microdrama *Pizza Guys vs Vampires*.

Adapt's model integrates advanced AI tools for transcription, translation, and voice generation with a curated network of native human linguists. This human-in-the-loop approach ensures that tone, humor, pacing, and emotional nuance are preserved so stories resonate authentically in each target language.

> "At Adapt, we are constantly improving our speed and quality to help our customers get their stories across the globe," says Matteo Natale, Chief Operating Officer of Adapt. "This collaboration demonstrates how AI, when paired with expert linguists, can unlock global distribution opportunities for emerging content formats like microdrama."

> "Today, global accessibility is essential for any content creator. Responsible implementation of AI requires responsible participation." — Mariko Nomura, Producer, Flashback Pictures

## About Flashback Pictures

Flashback Pictures is an innovative production company with nine original feature films slated over the next three years. Flashback's 'Moneyball' approach leverages undervalued incentives and overlooked talent to deliver studio-quality films at a fraction of traditional budgets. The company's first release, *Pizza Guys vs Vampires*, went from concept to launch in three months, earning a Latin America license deal and coverage in Deadline and StageRunner.

## About Adapt

Adapt is an AI-powered localization company that combines advanced technology with native linguists and Cultural Ambassadors to deliver high quality dubbing and translation services. The company supports studios, platforms, and brands looking to scale content globally with speed, accuracy, and cultural authenticity.
