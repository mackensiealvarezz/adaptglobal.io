*December 10, 2025 – Denver, CO*

Matteo Natale, Adapt's Chief Operating Officer, and Kevin Munro, Adapt's Chief Technology Officer, have been named to the EGA Association Top 100 Professional and Content Localization list, an industry recognition honoring leaders making a measurable impact across the localization industry.

Matteo Natale was recognized under the **Operations** category for his leadership in operational strategy, scalable delivery, and process optimization across global content and localization programs. As Chief Operating Officer, his work supports efficiency, quality, and sustainable growth across international markets.

Kevin Munro was recognized under the **Technology** category for his leadership in localization technology, systems innovation, and technical infrastructure that enables scalable, data-driven global content operations. As Chief Technology Officer, his contributions continue to advance how organizations deploy and manage localization technology at scale.

## About the EGA Association Top 100

The EGA Association Top 100 Professional and Content Localization list recognizes leading professionals across operations, technology, and content disciplines within the localization industry. The list serves as an annual benchmark for excellence, innovation, and leadership in global content and localization services.

## About Adapt

Founded in 2024, Adapt is a next-generation localization service provider that merges AI-driven transcription, translation, and voice generation with regional expertise to deliver high-quality dubs and subtitles for premium content — at half the cost and twice the speed of traditional providers.

Adapt's mission is to integrate AI with art and humans with technology, making content accessible to global audiences at the quality consumers demand and the efficiency creators need. We turn the acceptable into the exceptional.
