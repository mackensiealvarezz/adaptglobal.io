*December 10, 2025 – Denver, CO*

Matteo Natale, Adapt's Chief Operating Officer, and Kevin Munro, Adapt's Chief Technology Officer, have been named to the EGA Association Top 100 Professional and Content Localization list, an industry recognition honoring leaders making a measurable impact across the localization industry.

Matteo Natale was recognized under the **Operations** category for his leadership in operational strategy, scalable delivery, and process optimization across global content and localization programs. As Chief Operating Officer, his work supports efficiency, quality, and sustainable growth across international markets.

Kevin Munro was recognized under the **Technology** category for his leadership in localization technology, systems innovation, and technical infrastructure that enables scalable, data-driven global content operations. As Chief Technology Officer, his contributions continue to advance how organizations deploy and manage localization technology at scale.

## Their Contributions to Adapt's Growth

**Matteo Natale** joined Adapt as Chief Operating Officer bringing a track record of scaling localization operations at some of the industry's largest providers — Deluxe, Vubiquity, Prime Focus, and IDC. At Adapt, he has built the operational architecture that allows the company to simultaneously serve enterprise studios and independent creators, maintaining quality consistency across a workforce of Cultural Ambassadors and Tech Producers spread across six continents. His work includes establishing the project management framework, quality assurance pipelines, and vendor management systems that underpin Adapt's delivery guarantees.

**Kevin Munro** serves as Chief Technology Officer, overseeing the development of the Nuance platform — Adapt's proprietary localization workflow engine. His background spans cloud infrastructure engineering, security architecture, and product development at JUICE Inc. and Vubiquity. At Adapt, he is responsible for the technical integrations that connect AI engines from DeepL, ElevenLabs, Ooona, VoiceQ, and Pro Tools into a unified, trackable workflow. He also leads development of RealSync, Adapt's lip-sync alignment technology, and Resonance, the company's voice generation engine.

## The Value of Industry Recognition in a Fast-Moving Market

As AI localization matures from novelty to infrastructure, the professionals who lead these organizations play an increasingly important role in shaping how the technology is deployed responsibly. Industry recognition programs like the EGA Association Top 100 serve as an important external signal — helping clients, partners, and investors identify which organizations are building durable capabilities rather than riding a short-term trend.

## About the EGA Association Top 100

The EGA Association Top 100 Professional and Content Localization list recognizes leading professionals across operations, technology, and content disciplines within the localization industry. The list serves as an annual benchmark for excellence, innovation, and leadership in global content and localization services.
