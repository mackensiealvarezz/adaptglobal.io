*April 20, 2026 – Goalcast's Showdown en Français demonstrates the revenue potential of AI-driven localization at scale.*

Adapt, a global leader in AI-driven localization and dubbing solutions, today announced that Goalcast's newly launched French-language channel, Showdown en Français, generated 9 million views and 100,000 subscribers within its first weekend of launch. The channel was localized by Adapt using Dubformer's AI dubbing platform, with Facebook monetization thresholds crossed within days of going live.

Goalcast, which operates dozens of English-language YouTube and Facebook channels producing documentary, motivational, and scripted content, partnered with Adapt to expand its reach into French, Spanish, Arabic, Hindi, and German markets. Adapt's localization workflow combines Dubformer's AI dubbing technology with its international network of Cultural Ambassadors, specialists who direct voice, language, and delivery to ensure every adaptation resonates authentically with local audiences.

> "By combining Dubformer's technology with our proven Cultural Ambassador model, we can deliver localization that feels authentic to local audiences," said Justin Beaudin, CEO and Founder of Adapt. "You can always tell the dubbing is good when user comments are focused on the content itself rather than the dubbing. That's what we aim for."

The results reinforce what Adapt has consistently delivered across markets. When localization is executed with the right blend of technology and human expertise, content creators unlock monetization opportunities that were previously out of reach.
