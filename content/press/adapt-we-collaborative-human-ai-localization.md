*October 22, 2025*

Adapt is thrilled to announce its partnership with We Collaborative to accelerate innovation in human-driven, AI-enabled localization.

Adapt is redefining media localization by blending cutting-edge AI with the expertise of regional artists and linguists known as Cultural Ambassadors and Tech Producers. With over 20 years of experience scaling global localization operations, We Collaborative will help build the talent infrastructure that powers Adapt's technology — enhancing global resource pipelines, training programs, and continuous improvement initiatives.

> "I am very excited about this partnership," said Magda Jagucka Miller, Founder of We Collaborative. "As the media localization landscape continues to evolve, the Adapt team and I are at the forefront of developing new tools and processes. I'm passionate about designing future-fit solutions and helping individuals and organizations grow confidently and sustainably."

> "I'm thrilled to be working with Magda and We Collaborative. Adapt's strength comes from the linguists who guide it, and investing in their training is essential to our success." — Justin Beaudin, CEO, Adapt

Together, Adapt and We Collaborative will roll out new training programs, tools, and resources for over 500 Cultural Ambassadors worldwide in the coming months — empowering the next generation of global localization professionals.

## About Adapt

Adapt is dedicated to integrating AI with human expertise to elevate media services, making the acceptable exceptional. Founded in 2024, Adapt explores the intersection of technology and creativity in media.
